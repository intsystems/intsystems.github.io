### About

This course is dedicated to introduction to speciality. Students are introduced to modern challenges and advanced methods in the field of intelligent data analysis. The format includes weekly meetings with experts in the field, discussions of possible research topics, and guidance on finding a scientific supervisor.

### Grading

Final grade: pass / fail. Awarded based on whether the student has chosen a scientific supervisor and determined the direction of future research by the end of the semester.

### Prerequisites

- Mathematical analysis, linear algebra
- General physics, field theory
- Probability theory and mathematical statistics
- Set theory and logic
